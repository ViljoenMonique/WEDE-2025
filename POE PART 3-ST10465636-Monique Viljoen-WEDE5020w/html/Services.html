Our Services

At Bread for All Foundation, we believe no one should go to bed hungry. Our services focus on delivering nutritious meals, essential supplies, and long-term food support to vulnerable communities in Durban and surrounding areas.

Soup Kitchens

Our soup kitchens operate in multiple communities every week, offering warm, nutritious meals to those in need. We prioritise children, the elderly, and unemployed individuals who face daily food insecurity. Beyond just food, our kitchens are a place of dignity, compassion, and community.

Food Parcel Programmes

For families who cannot attend our soup kitchens, we distribute food parcels containing essentials such as maize meal, rice, beans, cooking oil, and tinned goods. These parcels help sustain households for a week or more, providing immediate relief while we work on long-term hunger solutions.

Our Goals

Primary Goals

  • Raise R50,000 in monthly donations through our secure donation portal to fund food purchases and logistics.
  • Recruit 30 volunteers per month using our online volunteer signup form to keep our programmes running efficiently.
  • Distribute 2,000 meals per month, tracked through our backend system and verified via Google Sheets integration.

Measuring Our Impact

Our monthly meal distribution goals are visualised using infographics on our website, so donors and supporters can see real progress at a glance. This transparency ensures that every supporter knows the difference their contribution makes.

By following nonprofit best practices, we focus on donor retention and community-building to create long-term solutions to hunger, not just temporary fixes.
